About CareerForge

Built to Close the
Preparation Gap

The challenge students face isn't a lack of talent — it's not knowing where their gaps are until it's too late.

The Problem We Solved

Every year, millions of students graduate and enter the job market with high hopes — but many discover their preparation gaps only after multiple failed interviews. At that point, the rejection has already happened, confidence is shaken, and recovery takes months.

The brutal truth: most students don't know what they don't know. They prepare in silos — grinding LeetCode, tweaking their resume, or doing a few mock interviews — but never get an objective, holistic view of their readiness.

Our Solution

AI CareerForge is a 2-minute interview readiness assessment that gives students an objective, multi-dimensional score across the four pillars that actually matter in interviews: Technical Skills, Resume Quality, Communication, and Portfolio visibility.

Instead of generic advice, we deliver a personalized action plan with prioritized steps ranked by impact — so students spend their limited prep time on what actually moves the needle.

The Four Dimensions

Our scoring model is built on research into what hiring managers at top tech companies actually evaluate during their recruitment process:

💻

Technical Skills (30%)

DSA proficiency, system design knowledge, framework experience, project complexity. This is the heaviest weighted dimension for engineering roles.

📄

Resume Quality (25%)

Structure, impact metrics, ATS optimization, completeness, and whether it's tailored to the target role. Your resume is your first impression with recruiters.

🎤

Communication (25%)

Mock interview experience, STAR method prep, behavioral question readiness, and verbal communication confidence. Often the differentiator between equally-skilled candidates.

💼

Portfolio & Visibility (20%)

GitHub activity, live projects, certifications, and LinkedIn presence. Increasingly, recruiters check digital presence before reaching out.
